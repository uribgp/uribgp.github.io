A B O U T M E

I am a passionate, innovate developer who is extremely competitive with himself and doesn't beleive in the phrase "If it ain't broke, don't fix it." As much as I love finding out of the box solutions, I love to understand, fine-tune and perfect what is already working even more. recent projects.

Since graduating college, I've been a professional poker player. I was originally drawn in by competitive aspect of quick, real money, high stress scenarios. I quickly realized turning that competitiveness inwards -- discovering, finetuning and perfecting small details about my game -- was the way to truly excel. It is This love for discovery and perfecting that I hope to bring to the development world.

I am an avid reader, hiker, gamer and writer. I'm currently working on translating an indie Chinese game to English, and I'm still holding on to my childhood dream of writing a movie script. I'm hoping to finish the script before the end of the year, and it will likely be adorning trashcans all over Hollywood soon after.
